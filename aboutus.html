About Us

Forging a Legacy: The Beaverton Beavers

In 1925, amid the serene beauty of Beaverton, a group of baseball enthusiasts, led by the spirited Harold "Homerun" Henderson, embarked on a mission: to create a team that would unite their community and make a mark in the world of baseball. With unwavering determination, they scoured the town for talent, rallied local support, and prepared for their debut season. In 1926, amidst cheers and anticipation, the Beaverton Beavers took the field for their first game, laying the foundation for a storied legacy. Through victories and defeats, the Beavers remained a symbol of resilience and unity, inspiring generations of fans with their passion for the game and dedication to their community. Today, as they continue to grace the diamond, the spirit of the founding members lives on, a testament to the enduring allure of America's favorite pastime.

The Golden Era: Glory on the Diamond

During the 1950s, the Beaverton Beavers experienced a golden era that left an indelible mark on the town's sporting history. Led by the legendary pitcher, Jack "Strikeout" Sullivan, and the power-hitting sensation, Sarah "Slugger" Johnson, the Beavers dominated the league, clinching multiple championships and thrilling fans with their electrifying performances. With Sullivan's pinpoint accuracy on the mound and Johnson's awe-inspiring home runs, the Beavers became synonymous with success, drawing crowds from far and wide to witness their remarkable feats on the diamond. Their rivalry with the neighboring Cascade Cougars added an extra layer of excitement to every game, fueling intense battles and unforgettable moments that are still talked about to this day. As the Beaverton Beavers basked in the glory of their triumphs, they cemented their place in the hearts of fans, becoming more than just a baseball team but a symbol of pride and unity for the entire community.

Beyond the Diamond: Community Outreach and Impact

Beyond their prowess on the baseball diamond, the Beaverton Beavers have long been champions of community outreach and social impact. From hosting youth clinics and baseball camps to partnering with local charities and organizations, the team has always been committed to giving back to the town that has supported them through thick and thin. Players regularly visit schools to promote the values of teamwork, perseverance, and sportsmanship, inspiring the next generation of athletes to reach for their dreams. The team's annual charity events, including fundraisers and community clean-up initiatives, have raised thousands of dollars for causes close to the hearts of Beaverton residents. Through their dedication to making a positive difference off the field, the Beaverton Beavers continue to embody the true spirit of sportsmanship and camaraderie, leaving a lasting legacy that extends far beyond the confines of the baseball diamond.
