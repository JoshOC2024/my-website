Beaverton Beavers

What are we about?

Meet the Beaverton Beavers, the hometown heroes of Beaverton! This dynamic baseball team embodies the spirit of their city, captivating fans with their skill and camaraderie on the field. With each pitch and swing, they ignite a sense of pride and unity, uniting the community in a shared love for the game. Go Beavers!

What To Bring!

The following are just some suggestions on what to bring when you show up to the games. Traveling heavy can become cumbersome show try and pack lightly:

  • Umbrella
  • Raincoat
  • Small clear bag
  • Beavers team gear
