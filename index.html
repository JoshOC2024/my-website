What are we about?

Meet the Beaverton Beavers, the hometown heroes of Beaverton! This dynamic baseball team embodies the spirit of their city, captivating fans with their skill and camaraderie on the field. With each pitch and swing, they ignite a sense of pride and unity, uniting the community in a shared love for the game. Go Beavers!

Excitement Builds for the 2024 Season

As the 2024 season approaches, anticipation is at an all-time high for Beaverton Baseball fans. This year's lineup features a blend of seasoned veterans and promising rookies, each bringing their unique skills and energy to the field. Highlights of the upcoming season include thrilling matchups against our long-time rivals, special themed game nights, and the much-awaited return of our star pitcher, who is back after an impressive recovery from injury. The management has also announced plans for a fan appreciation week, featuring exclusive giveaways and interactive events. With a renewed sense of determination and a commitment to excellence, Beaverton Baseball is poised to deliver an unforgettable season full of memorable moments and heart-stopping action.
